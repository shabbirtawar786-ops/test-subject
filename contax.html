Contax

“Simplify Tax Compliance. Smart, Secure, Seamless.”

TDS & Tax Management System, better known as ConTax, is a complete tax accounting and compliance solution designed to simplify TDS calculations, challan generation, and government return filing for organizations and professionals. Developed with precision and compliance expertise, ConTax ensures accurate tax deductions, transparent reporting, and effortless statutory filing—all from a single, intelligent, and secure platform.

See Features

Core Features

Smart Tax Tracking & Challan Generation:

Easily track TDS payments made to individuals, contractors, and professionals. Generate physical challans for bank deposits or initiate secure online payments directly through the system.

Minimum Input – Maximum Output:

Designed for efficiency—ConTax requires minimal data entry and automatically generates multiple reports and forms, saving time and reducing errors.

Comprehensive Tax Reports:

Generate all essential reports including Form-16A (Quarterly Provisional), Quarterly Returns, Checklists, and other compliance-ready documents—instantly and accurately.

Built-In Data Security:

Inbuilt security safeguards ensure complete confidentiality of taxpayer and payment data, accessible only to authorized personnel.

Automated Return Data Generation:

Prepare accurate data for quarterly income tax return filing with automated updates as and when new rules or notifications are released by the Income Tax Department.

Shortcut Keys for Speed:

Smart keyboard shortcuts enable instant data entry, verification, and report generation—enhancing productivity and reducing manual effort.

“Power Up Your Compliance with ConTax”

ConTax blends automation, security, and intelligence—helping organizations manage tax compliance effortlessly. It’s not just a TDS tool—it’s your trusted tax management partner for accuracy, compliance, and control.
